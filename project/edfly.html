About Us

At Edfly Study Abroad, we believe that every student deserves the opportunity to explore global education and build a successful future without boundaries. As a growing startup, we are dedicated to guiding students through every step of their international education journey with clarity, confidence, and care. We specialize in providing personalized counseling to help students choose the right country, university, and course based on their academic goals, interests, and budget. Our team ensures a smooth and transparent process—from university selection and application support to visa assistance and pre-departure guidance. At Edfly, we go beyond just admissions. We focus on creating pathways to global careers by offering expert advice, scholarship guidance, and continuous support even after students reach their destination. With a student-first approach, strong global connections, and a commitment to excellence, Edfly Study Abroad aims to become a trusted partner for students aspiring to study overseas.
